Here's a concise guide on how to install Our app:

1. Open the app store on your device (Google Play Store for Android or Apple App Store for iOS).
2. Search for the app you want to install by typing its name in the search bar.
3. Select the app from the search results.
4. Tap the "Install" (Android) or "Get" (iOS) button.
5. Once the installation is complete, tap "Open" to launch the app.
